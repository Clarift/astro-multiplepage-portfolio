At Clarift, our core engineering philosophy revolves around efficiency, speed, and zero-maintenance hosting. In a world where modern web development is often over-engineered with massive node modules and heavy JavaScript runtimes, we take a different path. 

For our public marketing and content sites, we choose **Astro**. It ships zero JavaScript to the client by default, resulting in sub-100ms loading speeds and perfect SEO scores out of the box.

For our backend architectures, we rely heavily on clean **PHP (7.4 to 8.x)** coupled with native **MySQL**. Why? Because it runs everywhere, requires virtually zero server configuration, and costs next to nothing to host on standard shared servers. Combined with secure payment gateways like **Stripe API**, we are able to build fully automated, highly profitable digital assets that require almost no daily technical supervision.
