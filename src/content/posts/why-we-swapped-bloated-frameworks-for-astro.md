Mobile page load speed is no longer just a technical metric—it is a core business metric. Google directly ranks faster pages higher (SEO), and conversion rates drop by 7% for every single second of delay.

Traditional setups (like heavy WordPress builders or bloated single-page React apps) send megabytes of JavaScript to mobile browsers, causing slow render times.

### Enter Astro: HTML-First Performance
At Clarift, we build our public-facing landing pages and portfolios with **Astro** to achieve perfect scores:

* **Zero JS by Default:** Astro delivers raw, fully-rendered HTML to the client browser. No loading spinners, no bloated client-side bundles.
* **Island Architecture:** If a page needs an interactive widget (like our live chat or custom sliders), Astro loads JavaScript *only* for that specific component (the "island"), leaving the rest of the page static and fast.
* **Flawless Google Lighthouse Scores:** Our builds consistently hit **100/100** on performance, SEO, accessibility, and best practices.

### Why It Matters for Your Business
When you buy a digital asset built with Astro, you get built-in SEO advantages. Your ad spend on platforms like TikTok and Facebook converts better because the landing page loads instantly (<200ms) on mobile phones.
