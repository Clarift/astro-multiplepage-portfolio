Building and launching lightweight, profitable web applications is what we do best. With DateMix 2.0 fully developed and ready for hand-off to marketing-focused buyers, our engineering studio is already looking at the horizon.

Here is a sneak peek at what we are building next at Clarift:
1. **Privacy-First "Link-in-Bio" for E-commerce:** An ultra-fast, cookieless landing page builder designed to help social media sellers showcase products and accept payments directly, without annoying GDPR banners.
2. **Automated "Scope Creep" Analyzer:** A SaaS utility for freelancers that integrates with Gmail and Slack to semantically analyze client requests against their original Statement of Work (SOW), automatically drafting assertive upsell proposals.

Stay tuned as we continue to push the boundaries of clean code and micro-SaaS!
